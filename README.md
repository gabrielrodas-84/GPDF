## 📖 What is GPDF?

GPDF is a **design pattern and operating model** for building governed, request-based
business processes using structured data, modular orchestration, and rule-driven
decisions.

It is designed for teams and organizations where business processes are currently
managed through emails, spreadsheets, or informal approvals — and where
**traceability, auditability, and consistent decision-making** are required.

GPDF does not replace enterprise transactional systems (ERP, ITSM). It operates as
a **governed decision layer** for processes that fall outside those platforms.

---

## 🏗️ Architecture

GPDF organizes every governed process into **six independent layers**:

| Layer | Responsibility |
|-------|---------------|
| **User** | Defines who interacts with the process and in what role (Requester, Reviewer, Approver, Executor, Process Owner) |
| **Data** | Single authoritative store for all requests, decisions, status changes, and evidence |
| **Orchestration** | Automated workflows that enforce process logic, transitions, validations, and handoffs |
| **Governance** | Business rules, approval matrices, and decision criteria managed as structured configuration data |
| **Intelligence** | Optional AI-assisted analysis, recommendations, and contextual support |
| **Output** | Notifications, reporting, dashboards, and audit logs |

Every process follows a standard lifecycle: **Intake → Decision → Execution → Closure**.

---

## 🎯 Key Outcomes

- ✅ Replacement of email-based approvals with structured, auditable decision flows
- ✅ Full traceability from request intake to closure
- ✅ Consistent execution through stage-based ownership and controlled handoffs
- ✅ Scalable process design through configuration, not re-engineering

---

## 🧱 Core Principles

- **Single Source of Truth** — Every process is anchored to one authoritative data store
- **Stage Ownership** — Each lifecycle phase has a defined owner with clear responsibilities
- **Rule-Driven Decisions** — Governance rules are managed as data, never hardcoded
- **Platform-Agnostic** — Works on Microsoft 365, ServiceNow, Google Workspace, SQL/Custom,
  or any stack that meets the requirements
